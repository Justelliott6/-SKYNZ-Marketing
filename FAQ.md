| Price | $199 one-time | $5-20/month | $19-99/month |
| Blockchain | Solana | None | None |

### Who is SKYNZ for?

SKYNZ is for any creator who wants to:
- Own their digital presence

This includes musicians, artists, streamers, podcasters, influencers, and any content creator.

## Revenue & Monetization

### How much revenue will I make?

Revenue depends on your audience and engagement. Here's an example:

**Scenario: Musician with 10,000 followers**
- Page sales: 100 fans × $199 = $19,900 (you keep $18,905)
- Secondary market: 50 resales × $500 avg = $25,000 (you keep $2,500 in royalties)
- Utility revenue: 10 fans × $200/year = $2,000 (you keep $1,900)
- **Total Year 1: $23,305**

Your actual revenue will vary based on your audience size and engagement.

### How do I get paid?

Payments are distributed:
- **Weekly:** Platform revenue share
- **Monthly:** Utility revenue
- **On sale:** Secondary market royalties

Payments are sent to your Solana wallet automatically.

### What's the revenue share?

**Primary Sale (You purchase your page):**
- You: 95% ($189)
- Platform: 5% ($10)

**Secondary Market (Fans buy/sell your page):**
- You: 10% royalty (enforced at contract level)
- Seller: 88% (after marketplace fee)
- Marketplace: 2%

**Utility Revenue (Annual):**
- You: 95% of utility revenue
- Platform: 5%

### Can I earn passive income?

Yes! SKYNZ offers multiple passive income streams:

1. **Secondary Market Royalties** — Earn 10% on every resale forever
2. **Platform Revenue Share** — Top-tier badge holders earn 2% of platform revenue
3. **Utility Revenue** — Earn from premium features purchased by fans

## Blockchain & Security

### What blockchain does SKYNZ use?

SKYNZ uses **Solana** because it's:
- Fast (transactions in seconds)
- Cheap (fees under $0.01)
- Green (energy-efficient)
- Scalable (handles millions of transactions)
- Established (trusted by major platforms)

### Is SKYNZ safe?

Yes! SKYNZ uses:
- **Anchor framework** — Battle-tested smart contract framework
- **Metaplex standard** — Industry-standard NFT format
- **Security audits** — Smart contracts audited for vulnerabilities
- **Royalty enforcement** — Enforced at contract level
- **Ownership verification** — On-chain verification before redemption

### Do I need to know about blockchain?

No! SKYNZ handles all blockchain complexity for you. You just:
1. Purchase your page
2. Receive an NFT in your wallet
3. Manage your page through our dashboard
4. Get paid automatically

### What wallet do I need?

You'll need a Solana wallet to:
- Purchase your page
- Receive payments
- Trade badges

Popular Solana wallets include:
- **Phantom** (recommended)
- **Solflare**
- **Magic Eden**
- **Ledger**

### Is my wallet secure?

Your wallet security depends on you. Best practices:
- Use a hardware wallet (Ledger) for large amounts
- Never share your private key
- Enable 2FA on your wallet
- Keep your seed phrase safe (write it down, store offline)

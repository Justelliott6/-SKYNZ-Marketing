## Presentation Tips

### For Investors
- Focus on market size and revenue potential
- Show the working MVP and prototypes
- Emphasize the competitive advantage
- Be clear about the ask and use of funds
- Show passion and commitment (8 months of work)

### For Creators
- Focus on benefits and ease of use
- Show the working dashboard with real content
- Emphasize ownership and revenue
- Make it personal (this is YOUR page, YOUR data, YOUR money)
- Show the multi-streaming power

### For Partners
- Focus on integration opportunities
- Show the API and technical architecture
- Emphasize the growing creator base
- Talk about revenue sharing and partnership models
- Show the roadmap and growth potential

### General Tips
- Practice your delivery
- Know your numbers cold
- Be ready for tough questions
- Show genuine passion
- Let the product speak for itself
- End with a clear call to action

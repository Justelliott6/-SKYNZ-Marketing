## 🎬 What is SKYNZ?

SKYNZ is a **Creator Command Page** platform that lets creators own their digital presence as NFTs on the Solana blockchain.

Instead of renting a page from a platform (like Linktree), creators **own their page** and earn **95% of all revenue** forever.

### The Problem We Solve

- ❌ Creators rent pages from platforms (Linktree, Carrd, Beacons)
- ❌ Platforms take 30-50% of revenue
- ❌ Creators have no ownership
- ❌ No passive income opportunities
- ❌ Limited customization options

### The SKYNZ Solution

- ✅ Creators **own their page as an NFT**
- ✅ Keep **95% of all revenue**
- ✅ Earn **10% royalty on secondary sales** forever
- ✅ Unlimited customization (5 premium themes)
- ✅ Passive income from platform revenue share
- ✅ Transferable & tradeable on Magic Eden & Tensor
